# CRM-CHAISHOP-CO-PROJECT
**CRM Application for TheChaiShop&Co**
**Project Overview**:
The CRM Application for TheChaiShop&Co is a Salesforce-based Customer Relationship Management system designed to manage customer interactions, automate sales processes, and provide business insights through reports and dashboards.

    This application helps sales representatives efficiently manage customer records, track orders, automate billing, and improve customer engagement through automated communication.

**Features**
**User Authentication**
Secure login system that allows authorized users to access the CRM application using valid credentials.

**Customer Account Management**:
Enables users to create, view, update, and manage customer details such as:
Customer Name
Contact Number
Address
Other relevant information

**Employer Account Management**:
Allows the organization to maintain and manage employer-related records within the CRM system.

**Enterprise Account Management**:
Supports management of enterprise-level business accounts and maintains organized records for larger business entities.

**Order Management**:
Allows sales representatives to create and track customer orders including:
Product details
Order quantity
Order information
Automated Billing
Automatically calculates the total bill amount using Salesforce formula fields, reducing manual errors and improving accuracy.

**Data Validation**:
Validation rules ensure correct data entry for important fields such as:
Phone Number
PAN
Aadhaar

**Record Types & Page Layouts**:
Different record types and page layouts are used to distinguish between various account types and improve data organization.

**Global Picklists**:
Standardized values are maintained for fields such as:
Product Category
Status
Product Type

**Email Notification System**:
Automated email notifications are sent to customers for:
Birthday wishes

**Workflow & Flow Automation**:
Salesforce Flows and Workflows automate tasks such as:
Record updates
Alerts and notifications

**Reports & Dashboards**:
The system provides interactive reports and dashboards to:
Monitor sales performance
Analyze customer data
Support business decision-making

**Data Security**:
Salesforce ensures secure cloud-based storage, backup, and recovery of business data.
Role-Based Access Control
Different profiles and permission sets control access levels for different users in the system.

**Technologies Used**:
Salesforce CRM
Salesforce Flow Automation
Formula Fields
Validation Rules
Reports and Dashboards
Record Types & Page Layouts

**Benefits of the System**:
Streamlines customer relationship management
Reduces manual errors in billing and data entry
Improves sales tracking and reporting
Enhances customer engagement through automated communication
Supports data-driven decision-making

**Conclusion**:
The CRM Application for TheChaiShop&Co demonstrates how Salesforce can be used to build an efficient CRM system that automates sales processes, manages customer data effectively, and provides valuable business insights through analytics.
